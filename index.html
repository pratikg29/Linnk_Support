Getting Started

What is LinkUp?

LinkUp is an app designed to help you maintain meaningful relationships with friends and family. It helps you track your interactions, set reminders for check-ins, and maintain regular contact with the people who matter most.

How do I add contacts?

To add a contact:

  1. Tap the "+" button on the home screen
  2. Select a contact from your address book
  3. Set a preferred check-in frequency
  4. Tap "Save" to add the contact

Features

Check-ins

Record your interactions with contacts by tapping "Link Up". Add notes to remember important conversations and moments.

Streaks

Build and maintain streaks by consistently checking in with your contacts according to your set frequency. Watch your streak grow as you maintain regular contact!

Statistics & Insights

Premium users can access detailed statistics about their relationships, including:

  • Weekly and monthly activity patterns
  • Contact frequency distribution
  • Top contacts and consistency scores

Frequently Asked Questions

How do notifications work?

LinkUp sends two types of notifications:

  • Check-in reminders based on your set frequencies
  • Birthday reminders (if birthdays are added to contacts)
How do I change a contact's check-in frequency?

Open the contact's detail page, scroll to settings, and tap on "Frequency" to choose a new check-in schedule.

Can I export my data?

Currently, data export is not available. We're working on adding this feature in a future update.

How do I restore my premium purchase?

Go to Settings, tap "Upgrade to Pro", and then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the screen.

Privacy & Data

LinkUp takes your privacy seriously. The app:

  • Stores all data locally on your device
  • Only accesses contacts with your permission
  • Never shares your personal information with third parties
  • Uses notifications only for reminders you've enabled
